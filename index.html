ISLAND HOPPING KITE SURFING FLYBOARD CHILL AND SPA PARTY

A brand new place to chill out in Boracay! With our young and dynamic atmosphere, Chill Out Hostel is a new haven for budget minded travellers from around the world, who want to enjoy the beauty and fun vibe of the world famous island of Boracay. Known for its 2 famous beaches, the White Beach with picture perfect sunsets and Bolabog Beach, world renowned for wind sports such as kite surfing and wind surfing.

Chill Out is located 8 minute walk to White Beach and 1 minute away from Bolabog Beach.

Our mixed aircon/non-aircon dorm rooms offer you maximum privacy with individual bed curtains, lockers big enough to fit your backpack, ensuite hot & cold showers with free shampoo, shower gel and bath towels, cleanliness at its best.

Chill Out Hostel provides affordable food and drinks at our Chill Restobar, free WiFi lounge area with a wide screen TV, different activities such as darts and more. Save your time and money by checking our Chill Out info board for things to do in Boracay, getting around, night life and events around the island.

CHILL ESCAPE ENJOY
  • Free rum nights. Yes, free. Rum. From 7-9pm. Let’s party.
  • Friday family meal night. One of the highlights of island life is fresh fish. And it tastes best grilled.
  • Free 15 minute kitesurfing trial when it’s windy.
  • Free night when you take kitesurfing or diving lessons.
  • Discounts on different activities, like island hopping and water sports.

FIND US

Location

Chill Out is in the area called Balabag, located 8 minute walk to White Beach and 1 minute away from Bolabog Beach. It's a quiet leafy area, perfect to escape the bustle and with cheap restaurants on the same road.

Directions

We are on on Bulabog Road. Take a trike or motorbike to Iglesia Ni Cristo Church. We are next to our friends at Cool Stay Inn.
